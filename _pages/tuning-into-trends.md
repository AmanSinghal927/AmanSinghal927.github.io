**Presented by:** Aman Singhal, Karthikeyan Shanmugam, Stanley Jovel

**Venue:** Big Data Analytics Symposium, NYU Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences

**Year:** 2024

## Abstract

This talk presents an analysis of the relationship between artists' streaming popularity, lyrical emotion, and social media sentiment. We explore how these factors interconnect and influence music consumption patterns in the digital age.
