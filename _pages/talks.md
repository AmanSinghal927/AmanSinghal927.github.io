## Large Language and Vision Models Symposium, NYU Center for Data Science (2024)

[GPT-3: Language Models are Few-shot Learners](/talks/gpt3-few-shot-learners/) - Overview of GPT-3's 175 billion parameter model and its few-shot learning capabilities without task-specific fine-tuning.

[Llama 2: Open Foundation and Fine-Tuned Chat Models](/talks/llama2-open-foundation/) - Meta's open-source LLM with dual reward models for balancing helpfulness and safety through RLHF.

[Generative Agents: Interactive Simulacra of Human Behavior](/talks/generative-agents/) - AI-powered characters that simulate believable human behavior using memory, reflection, and planning architectures.

[CLIP: Learning Transferable Visual Models from Natural Language Supervision](/talks/clip-visual-models/) - Vision-language model trained on 400 million image-text pairs for zero-shot transfer to downstream tasks.

## Big Data Analytics Symposium, NYU Courant Institute (2024)

[Tuning into Trends: An Analysis of Artists' Streaming Popularity](/talks/tuning-into-trends/) - Analysis of streaming popularity, lyrical emotion, and social media sentiment in the music industry.
